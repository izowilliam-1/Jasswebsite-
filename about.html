Our History

Rooted in Munuki

Juba Adventist Secondary School was established to provide Seventh-day Adventist education to the Munuki community and beyond, combining national academic standards with the values of the Adventist faith. From a small founding cohort, the school has grown into a center of learning known for discipline, character and service in Juba.

Today JASS continues to welcome students of all backgrounds who are seeking an education that develops not only the mind, but the whole person.
